Organic Heirloom Vegetables & Broth Sourcing

By Farm-to-Table Culinary Agronomist • 8 Min Read

Commercial vegetables grown with synthetic fertilizers lack the rich natural sugars and minerals needed for deep comfort broth bases. **Organic Heirloom Root Vegetables** (sweet Nantes carrots, golden parsnips, and red shallots) provide natural sweetness. This guide explores farm-to-table sourcing.

1. Heirloom Root Vegetable Caramelization

Roasting organic root vegetables before adding them to braising pots caramelizes natural fructose, imparting sweet undertones to savory stews.

2. 24-Hour Roasted Bone Marrow Stock

Roasting grass-fed beef marrow bones and simmering them for 24 hours extracts gelatin, calcium, and deep umami minerals.
